The major problem faced by majority of the Indian households is the water wastage and the mismanagement of water , due to the usage of water pumps in our homes, major points are listed below:-

Problem of water wastage:

One of the major reason for developing this project was to stop the enormous amount of water wastage which happens every day in our country, It's quite common that you see overflowing water from the water tanks because we just forget to turn off the water pump.

Problem of accessibility :

Accessibility is also one of the major problem, In this modern world of phone and technology we all want to control our appliances from the touch of our mobile screen, we all most of the time forget to switch on the water pump on time and then we want someone else to switch it on.

Problem of energy wastage:

Due to the over usage the pump, so much of electricity is get wasted which can be saved by using this product, In our household and any of our industries or any government based office.

The working of this system is based on various IOT and relay based mechanism which are listed below in various points:

Working of power on system:

Power on system is basically working on the IOT model of Arduino, so the main switch of water pump is connected to the relay switch which is connected to a ESP32 microcontroller , the ESP control the relay when it get the signal to switch it on from the app or your mobile phone, once the relay get on it switch on the main water pump power supply.

Working of power off system:

Power off system works on a circuit breaker mechanism , the device is connected to a float valve switch which switch on when the water is filled in the tank , it switch on the relay switch in the device which cut off the main power supply and the water pump is turned off.

Working of smart pump:

The smart pump is the combination of both power on and power off mechanism which make it a complete package, Once you switch on the water pump from your mobile phone the pump will get automatically turn off.
